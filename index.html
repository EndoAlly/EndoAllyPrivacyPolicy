2. Data Storage and Protection

We prioritize the security and confidentiality of your sensitive health information.

  • Health Data Storage (Local): All sensitive **Symptom Entry** and **Health Profile** data is stored **locally on your device** using **SwiftData**. This data is **not** synced to our cloud servers and is secured by the security features of your iOS device (e.g., device passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID).
  • **Authentication Data Storage (Cloud):** Your **Email Address** and **Hashed Password** are stored securely on **Google Firebase servers** in encrypted format for authentication purposes.
  • **Data Sharing:** **We do not sell or rent your personal or health data to any third parties for marketing, advertising, or commercial purposes.**

3. Third-Party Services (Firebase)

We utilize third-party services for essential functions and security:

  • Authentication and Backend: We use **Google Firebase** to handle user authentication and manage your account data.
  • Firebase Privacy Policy: You can review the privacy policy for Google Firebase and related Google services here: https://policies.google.com/privacy?hl=en-US

4. User Rights and Data Deletion

  • **Access and Modification:** You can view, modify, and delete all of your saved symptom and profile data directly within the application.
  • **Account Deletion (Cloud Data):** If you wish to delete your account, please contact us at the support email provided below. Deleting your account will permanently remove your email address and authentication details from our Firebase servers.
  • **Local Data Deletion:** Deleting the app from your device will automatically delete the local **SwiftData** container, permanently removing all your stored symptom and profile entries.
